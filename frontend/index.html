MediPredict analyzes 8 critical biological indicators identified by health researchers to determine early onset diabetes risk.

Pregnancies & Age

Demographic factors indicating gestational diabetes history and age-related metabolic shifts.

Glucose & Insulin

Direct measures of blood sugar concentration and the body's pancreatic hormone responses.

Blood Pressure & BMI

Cardiovascular health markers and body composition metrics associated with metabolic syndrome.

Pedigree Function

A score mapping family history of diabetes, estimating the genetic hereditary components of risk.
